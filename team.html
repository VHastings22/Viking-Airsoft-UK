Meet the Team

The Viking Airsoft team is a tight-knit group of players, techs and field regulars who’ve grown the brand through honesty, reliability and a shared obsession with airsoft.

From CQB hammers to woodland DMRs, each member of the team brings their own strengths, styles and personalities to the field. What unites everyone is the same thing: a commitment to playing hard, staying humble and supporting the wider airsoft community.

Whether it’s representing the brand at events, testing new builds, running site days or just bringing the energy every weekend, the Viking Airsoft team is the backbone of the entire project.
