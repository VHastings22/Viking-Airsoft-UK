About Viking Airsoft UK

Viking Airsoft UK was born from the same place most good tech work starts: long nights at the workbench, a box of broken rifles and a refusal to accept “that will do” as an answer.

From routine servicing to full internal builds, the focus is simple – reliable, repeatable performance that survives the mud, rain and chaos of a proper airsoft day out.

The Viking brand is about rugged kit, honest advice and work that speaks for itself on the field, not just in photos.

Values

  • Honest recommendations – no unnecessary parts.
  • Field-tested performance, not just spec sheets.
  • Clear communication and realistic lead times.
